A modern, professional web-based **budget and banking management application** built using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**.  
Track your monthly salary, savings, spending, manage multiple accounts, view history, and generate reports in **PDF and Excel**.

---

## Features

- **Dashboard**: Visual overview of salary, saved amount, spending, and money left with an interactive chart.  
- **Budget Management**: Set monthly salary, choose savings, and spending amounts.  
- **Accounts**:  
  - Add, edit, and delete multiple accounts  
  - Choose bank type (BML, MIB, CBM)  
- **Transfers**: Log account-wise transfers automatically from saved budget.  
- **History**:  
  - Monthly budget history  
  - Account-wise transfer history  
  - Reset history option  
- **Reports**: Export budget history as **PDF** or **Excel**.  
- **Responsive Design**: Works on both **desktop** and **mobile** screens.
